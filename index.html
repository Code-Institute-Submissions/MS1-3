We are Vigilant

We offer emergency callouts for clients in Caterham, Banstead, Oxted and Warlingham. Our team supplies free quotes and advice for domestic and commercial clients in Surrey, London and the Home Counties, plus nationwide locations. We install and repair a wide-range of electronic systems, such as CCTV models and security alarms for nationwide clients. We provide a 12-month warranty on all products we install, including burglar alarms and access control systems. Our security company is accredited by the National Security Inspectorate (NSI) and Constructionline. Public liability insurance covers installations and maintenance services.
