Fire

The purpose of fire alarms is to inform the user, building manager or fire departments of the potential or actual fire. Smoke reacts to changes in particulate concentration (smoke) and thermal to temperature changes in the environment. In the event of smoke or temperature rise, the sensors are activated and transmit status information to the system. The system sends a message about the potential or actual danger to the user, informs the security services, activates the fire extinguishing systems, etc.
